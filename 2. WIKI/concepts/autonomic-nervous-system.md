# Autonomic Nervous System (ANS)

**Summary**: The branch of the peripheral nervous system that regulates the involuntary functions of internal organs, smooth and cardiac muscle, and glands. Comprises two largely opposing but complementary divisions: the **[[sympathetic-nervous-system]]** ("fight or flight") and the **[[parasympathetic-nervous-system]]** ("rest and digest"). Together they continuously fine-tune heart rate, breathing, digestion, blood pressure, temperature, and immune tone. In the [[gut-brain-axis]] and [[brain-skin-axis]], the ANS — particularly the [[vagus-nerve]] — is the principal neural conduit between organs and the brain.

**Aliases**: ANS, autonomic system, vegetative nervous system, 자율신경계

**Sources**: Autonomic Nervous System - Crash Course Anatomy & Physiology 13.md (Hank Green, Crash Course); How To Activate Your Parasympathetic Nervous System For Healing.md (Alex Howard); Why Your Child's Allergies & Eczema Keep Coming Back.md

**Source dates**: Crash Course educational content, undated; others undated

**Last updated**: 2026-04-23

> **자율신경계(autonomic nervous system, ANS)** 는 내장·평활근·심장근·분비샘의 **비수의적 기능**을 조절하는 말초신경계의 한 갈래다. **[[sympathetic-nervous-system|교감신경계]]**("싸우거나 도망")과 **[[parasympathetic-nervous-system|부교감신경계]]**("쉬고 소화")의 **두 축**이 같은 장기를 서로 반대 방향으로 조절한다. 둘은 항상 동시에 작동하며, **균형(비율)이 생리 상태를 결정**한다. 해부학적 차이 3가지 — (1) **기원**: 교감=흉요추(T1~L2), 부교감=뇌간·천골(craniosacral). (2) **신경절 위치**: 교감=척수 근처(광범위 방송), 부교감=표적 장기 근처(정밀 제어). (3) **섬유 길이**: 교감=전짧고 후길다, 부교감=전길고 후짧다. [[gut-brain-axis|장-뇌 축]]과 [[brain-skin-axis|뇌-피부 축]]의 주요 신경 통로는 **[[vagus-nerve|미주신경]]**. **만성 교감 우세**("maladaptive stress response")는 치유를 방해하며, [[atopic-dermatitis|AD]]·알레르기·면역 과민과 연관된다는 비공식 틀이 있으나 특정 임상 주장은 주의가 필요하다.

## The two divisions

The ANS is built as a pair of opposing systems — described by Crash Course as **foils** (source: Autonomic Nervous System Crash Course.md, evidence: educational-reference):

- **[[sympathetic-nervous-system]]** — "fight or flight". Amps the body up for emergency or stress.
- **[[parasympathetic-nervous-system]]** — "rest and digest". Conserves energy, supports maintenance, digestion, repair.

Both divisions **serve the same organs** but produce opposite effects — racing heart vs calm heart, dilated pupils vs constricted pupils, suspended digestion vs active digestion. At any moment, both are active; the **balance between them** determines physiologic state.

> *요약: ANS는 **서로 반대되는 짝**으로 설계된다. 교감(싸우거나 도망) vs 부교감(쉬고 소화). 두 계통이 **같은 장기에 작용하지만 반대 효과**를 낸다(심박수·동공·소화). 어느 순간에도 둘 다 활성이며, **둘 사이 균형**이 생리 상태를 결정한다.*

## Three anatomical differences

### 1. Origins

- **Sympathetic = thoracolumbar**: fibers originate between the thoracic vertebrae (where ribs attach) and the lumbar vertebrae.
- **Parasympathetic = craniosacral**: fibers originate from the base of the brain (cranial — including the [[vagus-nerve]]) and from the sacral spinal cord.

The parasympathetic origins "frame" the sympathetic origins, which is why it's called para-sympathetic ("beside the sympathetic") (source: Autonomic Nervous System Crash Course.md, evidence: educational-reference).

### 2. Ganglia location

Both divisions use **two neurons** per pathway, connecting at a **ganglion** (cluster of neuron cell bodies).

- **Sympathetic ganglia**: near the spinal cord. Allows one spinal signal to branch to many target organs simultaneously — a "Bat Signal" broadcast fit for emergency responses.
- **Parasympathetic ganglia**: near or inside their target organs. Allows specific, one-on-one control of individual organs — "batphone" for specific digestive or housekeeping tasks.

### 3. Fiber lengths

- **Sympathetic**: short preganglionic, long postganglionic. (Short to nearby ganglion; long out to distant effector.)
- **Parasympathetic**: long preganglionic, short postganglionic. (Long out to ganglion near target; short final step to effector.)

These anatomic patterns produce the functional signatures: sympathetic = fast, broad, whole-body; parasympathetic = slow, specific, targeted.

> *요약: 해부학적 차이 3가지 — (1) **기원**: 교감=흉요추, 부교감=두개천골(craniosacral, 뇌간 + 천골). (2) **신경절 위치**: 교감=척수 근처(Bat Signal 방송형), 부교감=표적 장기 근처(batphone 정밀형). (3) **섬유 길이**: 교감=전짧·후길, 부교감=전길·후짧. 결과 — 교감은 **빠르고 광범위·전신**, 부교감은 **느리고 특정·표적**.*

## Why this matters systemically

The ANS is the **bidirectional cable** between brain and body:

- **In the [[gut-brain-axis]]**: vagal parasympathetic afferents (sensory) carry microbial signals to the brain; parasympathetic efferents regulate gut motility, digestion, and immune tone. Sympathetic activation during stress shifts gut motility, permeability, and microbial composition (see [[dysbiosis]]).
- **In the [[brain-skin-axis]]**: chronic sympathetic dominance is implicated in [[atopic-dermatitis]] flares, allergy, and immune hypersensitivity (source: Why Your Child's Allergies & Eczema Keep Coming Back.md, evidence: expert-opinion, low rigor — promotional). Balanced parasympathetic tone is framed as supporting immune tolerance and barrier function.
- **In the [[hpa-axis]]**: the ANS intersects with the HPA (cortisol) axis to form the body's integrated stress response.

> *요약: ANS는 **뇌-몸 양방향 케이블**. (1) [[gut-brain-axis|장-뇌 축]]: 미주 부교감 구심성이 미생물 신호를 뇌로 운반, 원심성이 장 운동·소화·면역을 조절. 스트레스의 교감 활성이 장 운동·투과성·미생물 조성을 바꿈. (2) [[brain-skin-axis|뇌-피부 축]]: 만성 교감 우세가 AD 악화·알레르기·면역 과민과 연관(증거 수준 낮음·판매성 주의). 균형 잡힌 부교감 긴장도는 면역 관용·장벽 기능을 지원. (3) [[hpa-axis|HPA]]와 ANS가 교차하여 통합 스트레스 반응을 구성.*

## "Maladaptive stress response" framing

Alex Howard and others popularize the idea that **chronic sympathetic dominance** — stuck in fight/flight/freeze — impairs healing (source: How To Activate Your Parasympathetic Nervous System For Healing.md, evidence: expert-opinion, low rigor).

The framing:
- Acute stress response is normal and adaptive.
- **Normalizing** to chronic dysregulation (childhood trauma, chronic anxiety, self-medication) means the body rarely enters parasympathetic recovery state.
- Healing — cuts, broken bones, emotional trauma — occurs in parasympathetic state.
- **Therapeutic goal**: train the nervous system to return to parasympathetic tone through practices like meditation, yoga, tai chi, qigong, breathwork.

This framework is popular and mechanistically plausible, but **specific clinical claims should be evaluated with caution**. See [[parasympathetic-activation]] protocol.

> *요약: **"부적응 스트레스 반응" 틀**(Alex Howard 등) — 만성 교감 우세(fight/flight/freeze에 갇힘) → 치유 저해. 핵심 주장 — (1) 급성 스트레스는 정상·적응적. (2) 아동기 트라우마·만성 불안·자가 투약으로 **만성 조절 장애에 정상화**되면 부교감 회복 상태에 드물게 진입. (3) 치유(상처·뼈·정서 외상)는 부교감 상태에서 일어남. (4) 치료 목표 — 명상·요가·태극권·기공·호흡으로 부교감 긴장도 훈련. 기전은 그럴듯하나 **특정 임상 주장은 신중 평가 필요**.*

## Modulating the ANS

- **[[parasympathetic-activation]]** practices — see protocol page.
- **[[acupuncture]]** — peripheral nerve stimulation via acupoints is shown to modulate brain activity and pain perception; auricular acupuncture is specifically investigated for vagal activation.
- **Pharmacologic** — vagal nerve stimulation (implanted or transcutaneous) is established for epilepsy and depression; being investigated for inflammatory conditions.

> *요약: ANS 조절 방법 — (1) [[parasympathetic-activation|부교감 활성화]] 실천(프로토콜 페이지 참조). (2) [[acupuncture|침]] — 경혈의 말초신경 자극이 뇌 활동·통증 지각을 조절. 이침(귀 침)은 미주 활성화로 특별히 연구됨. (3) **약물학적** — 미주신경 자극(이식형·경피형)이 뇌전증·우울증에 확립, 염증성 질환에 연구 중.*

> [!tip]+ 핵심 정리
> 1. **자율신경계 = 비수의 기능의 조절자**. 교감(fight-or-flight) + 부교감(rest-and-digest)의 반대 짝.
> 2. **같은 장기, 반대 효과**. 어느 순간에도 둘 다 작동. **균형(비율)이 상태를 결정**.
> 3. **해부학 3가지 차이**: 기원·신경절 위치·섬유 길이. 교감=방송형, 부교감=표적형.
> 4. **장-뇌·뇌-피부 축의 주요 신경 통로는 미주신경**(부교감).
> 5. **만성 교감 우세**는 치유·면역 관용·장벽 기능에 부정적 영향 — AD 관련 틀에서 자주 언급되나 특정 임상 주장은 신중하게 해석.
> 6. **조절 경로**: 부교감 활성화 실천, 침, 미주신경 자극(VNS) 등.
