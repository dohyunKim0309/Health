## Concepts

### Skin / Atopic Dermatitis

**Hub**

- [[atopic-dermatitis]] — chronic inflammatory skin disease; triad of barrier dysfunction, type 2 inflammation, and pruritus.

**Skin barrier**

- [[skin-barrier-dysfunction]] — stratum corneum failure; TEWL, ceramides, NMF, antimicrobial peptides.
- [[filaggrin]] — structural protein; FLG mutations are a major AD genetic risk factor.

**Immune mechanism (type 2 / Th2 axis)**

- [[th2-inflammation]] — the Th2 cascade.
- [[epithelial-alarmins]] — TSLP, IL-25, IL-33.
- [[tslp]] — master upstream driver.
- [[il-4-and-il-13]] — shared IL-4Rα + IL-13Rα1 receptor; target of [[dupilumab]].
- [[il-31]] — canonical "itchy cytokine".
- [[jak-stat-signaling]] — intracellular convergence point.
- [[ige]] — antibody class; downstream.

**Itch / neurology**

- [[pruritus]] — peripheral + central itch.
- [[brain-skin-axis]] — sensory nerves → spinal cord → brain.
- [[active-inference-and-chronic-pain]] — Friston's predictive-processing model; applies to chronic itch.

**Comorbidities**

- [[atopic-dermatitis-mental-health]] — depression, anxiety, ADHD, cognitive impairment.
- [[sleep-disturbance-in-atopic-dermatitis]] — nocturnal itch, REM loss, self-amplifying loop.

**Drugs**

- [[dupilumab]] — anti-IL-4Rα monoclonal.

### Gut & Microbiome

**Hub**

- [[gut-brain-axis]] — bidirectional gut-CNS communication.
- [[gut-microbiome]] — community, function, composition.

**Core concepts**

- [[vagus-nerve]] — principal neural conduit; parasympathetic workhorse.
- [[short-chain-fatty-acids]] — fiber-fermentation products; Treg expanders.
- [[dysbiosis]] — stable altered microbial state.
- [[leaky-gut]] — intestinal permeability.
- [[zonulin]] — tight-junction regulator.
- [[microbial-cloud]] — person-to-person microbial transmission via air and surfaces.

**Immune cells**

- [[t-regulatory-cells]] — Foxp3+ suppressors.
- [[th17-cells]] — IL-17-producing pro-inflammatory.

**Therapies**

- [[fecal-microbiota-transplantation]] — whole-community transfer.
- [[probiotics]] — strain-specific effects; mixed evidence.

**Diseases**

- [[irritable-bowel-syndrome]] — functional GI disorder.
- [[multiple-sclerosis]] — autoimmune CNS; microbiome-dependent.
- [[autism-spectrum-disorder]] — gut-modulated behaviors in BTBR model.

### Sleep

**Hub / architecture**

- [[sleep-architecture]] — N1/N2/N3/REM, cycles, spindles, hypnogram.
- [[rem-sleep]] — dream-rich, memory consolidation, emotional processing.
- [[slow-wave-sleep]] — deep N3; physical repair + glymphatic clearance.

**Regulation**

- [[two-process-model-of-sleep]] — sleep pressure + circadian rhythm; SleepWake app.
- [[circadian-rhythm]] — 24-hour internal clock.
